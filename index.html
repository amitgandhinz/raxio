RAX.IO 2014

Level Up!

Building on the thriving technical community within Rackspace, and the success of RAX.IO 2013, Rackspace presents RAX.IO 2014 - Level Up!!

RAX.IO is a series of presentations, discussions, and workshops to help Rackers build better, stronger, faster technical teams.

RAX.IO is a marketplace where people freely move between sessions and conversations: listening, sharing, and shopping for information and ideas they are interested in.

RAX.IO is a summit, festival, extravaganza, barnraising, conference, unconference, amazing experience created by Rackers, for Rackers.

Now that we're all here, let's collaborate.

Unconference planning and voting time starts each morning before the plenary talks.

A multi-track unconference schedule is posted during lunch, with session slots running in lock-step with conference talks to make it easy to move between conference talks and unconference sessions.

Information about the hotel, hotel shuttles and Austin shuttles will be posted soon.

After two days of learning, sharing and relationship building, it's time to get down to the brass tacks and collaborate on code. The fourth and final day of RAX.IO is a day of workshops and hacking. More details will be posted soon.

Links to conference talk live streams and video recordings will be posted here.

Local Tech Crews are holding RAX.IO Re-Caps in each office to share take-aways and continue discussions locally. Join (or create!) your local crew and help make this happen.
